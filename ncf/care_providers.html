Care Providers

A care provider gives healthcare and support to those in need within local communities. They will be registered with the Care Quality Commission (CQC).

Care providers are set to feel particular strain during the coronavirus outbreak, due to extra users and staffing gaps caused by the illness.

Care settings who can join the National Care Force include:

  • Care homes
  • Home care service providers
  • Local authority social care services

How to get help

After passing our verification checks, you can post as many shifts as you have available to our platform.

A pool of registered healthcare professionals and volunteers will be matched to your shifts, depending on the nature of the work and skills required.

You will be able to choose a person to work the shift from your candidates, who will have been pre-vetted for experience, qualifications and background.
