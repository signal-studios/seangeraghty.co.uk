Healthcare Workers

A healthcare worker is someone who is qualified to work under any of these professional titles:

  • Doctor
  • Nurse
  • Care Worker
  • Support Worker
  • Physiotherapist
  • Occupational Therapist

If you work as any of these, you can sign up to the National Care Force as a healthcare worker.

How do I get involved?

Once you've signed up to the National Care Force and have passed compliance screening, you'll be able to apply for shifts available on the platform.

This will mean covering clinical staff rota gaps in a variety of settings, from care homes to local authority services.

Such work will support fellow care professionals and ease the strain to services caused by extra users and staffing gaps during coronavirus.
