About The Lamp

The Hoxton Lamp was born out of the idea of creating an adaptable light that could literally be used anywhere (as long as there was a socket nearby). The basic idea is the lamp can be hung up (like a pendant) or even chucked on your desk to provide you with a working light (that isn’t too overpowering or focussed). The Hoxton Lamp is 3D printed which means that it’s complex form can be manufactured in one piece ensuring structural integrity is maintained throughout the design.

Black / White

The Hoxton Lamp will come in two colour variations. Choose between a glossy arctic white or a matte charcoal black.
